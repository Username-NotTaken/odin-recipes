Meatloaf

Description

This hearty meatloaf is a comforting, classic dinner with a flavourful twist. Made from a blend of ground beef, breadcrumbs, sautéed onions, and savoury seasonings, it’s shaped into a tender loaf and baked to perfection. What sets this version apart is the rich, tangy topping: a modified ketchup glaze made by blending ketchup with a hint of brown sugar, a dash of Worcestershire sauce, and a touch of smoked paprika. This sauce caramelizes beautifully in the oven, adding a sweet and smoky depth that complements the juicy meatloaf beneath.

Perfect for a family dinner or leftovers the next day, this meatloaf balances traditional comfort with bold flavour. Serve it alongside mashed potatoes, roasted vegetables, or a fresh salad to round out the meal. Whether you're learning to cook or building your first recipe site, this dish is a simple, reliable staple worth sharing.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 pounds of ground beef
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup dried bread crumbs or finely crushed crackers
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 2/3 cup ketchup
  • 4 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons prepared mustard
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice or vinegar

    • I use cheddar Goldfish crackers

Directions

  1. Gather the ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x5-inch loaf pan.
  2. Combine ground beef, onion, milk, bread crumbs, and egg in a large bowl; season with salt and pepper. Transfer into prepared loaf pan.
  3. Mix ketchup, brown sugar, and mustard together in a small bowl until well combined; pour over meatloaf and spread it evenly over the top.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until no longer pink in the center, about 1 hour.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
